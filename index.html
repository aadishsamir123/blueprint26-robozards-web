PROJECT OVERVIEW

Our project is a multi-sensor smart home system designed to improve household safety and day-to-day monitoring through real-time alerts.

SYSTEM MODULES

  • Fire Alarm Detection: Thermistor-based temperature sensing for early heat rise alerts.
  • Earthquake Detection: Tilt switch sensing to detect unusual movement and vibration events.
  • Doorbell Alert: Ultrasonic sensor-based approach detection for visitor notifications.
  • Garden and Laundry Monitoring: Moisture and water sensors to prevent over/under watering and protect clothes from rain.
